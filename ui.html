Quick sketching



First sketches of creating a UI from scratch. This particular example would allow the user to select From one or more options and click a button to proceed.

Robust second iteration



Lots of improvements to the second iteration. Figma assets were provided by the client and this was created to integrate seamlessly into their current version. The problem? It turns out the app will soon be overhauled. As the client realized more of what they were looking for, I could create a more accurate third iteration.

Final design



The final design is much more minimal but is more modular- That is, it fits with a wider variety of what ever changes the client would like to make with their app in the future. Research suggested that a minimal style was better for onboarding.
